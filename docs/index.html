What is our objective?

We are a team of young hackers who want to raise awareness about the challenges that students can face in university life.
We tackle this problem through the gamification of different university life aspects.


Up4TheTask


Players have to simultaneously manage the four main aspects of student life; grades, nutrition, sleep, and social interaction.

There are a variety of 'powerups' that players can obtain, which can grant bonuses but can also have tradeoffs. Choose wisely!

Students have to juggle many activities throughout their 4 years of education. Can you survive long enough to graduate?
